My Mount Rainier Climbing Experience
An Article by John Klein

Hover over a photo to read about the picture.

Photographer Dave Morrow was clever enough to capture the dense center of the Milky Way projected above Mount Rainier. The human eye cannot see this many stars but a camera set to an exposure of 30 seconds on a clear night captures the impossibly massive galaxy.

Before ascending the volcanic Mt. Rainier we witnessed the panoramic lushness of the surrounding Puyallup River Valley.

Rays of the sunset illuminate an altocumulus cloud jettisoned from the sky into Mt. Rainier's peak. The glacier-fed White River floodplain over the eons has etched a path that winds down along the stratovolcano.
